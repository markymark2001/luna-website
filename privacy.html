Privacy Policy

MediaKey Solutions Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Taia mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), full name, and profile information when you create an account
  • Authentication Data: When you use third-party sign-in services (Google or Apple), we receive your email address, name, and authentication tokens
  • Birth Chart Data: Birth date, birth time, birth location (city, coordinates, timezone), gender, and name for astrological calculations
  • Chat Messages: Your conversations with our AI assistant, including questions, responses, and conversation context
  • Subscription Information: Purchase history, subscription status, trial information, and payment data (processed by Apple/Google, we do not store payment card details)
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, operating system, push notification tokens for delivering notifications
  • Onboarding Data: Your preferences and interests collected during the onboarding process

1.2 Technical and Diagnostic Information

We automatically collect certain technical information to maintain and improve the App:

  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Performance data
  • Device information for debugging purposes

1.3 Analytics and Usage Data

We collect analytics data to understand how you use the App and to improve your experience:

  • Event Data: Actions you take in the App (e.g., button clicks, feature usage, screen views)
  • User Context: Your user identifier, subscription status, and onboarding completion status
  • Feature Flags: Data about which features are enabled or disabled for your account
  • Session Information: App sessions and usage patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To create and maintain your account, deliver personalized astrological insights, and provide AI-powered chat responses
  • Process Subscriptions: To manage your subscription, process payments, and provide access to premium features
  • Personalization: To customize your experience based on your birth chart data and preferences
  • Communication: To send you notifications, updates, and important information about the App
  • App Improvement: To understand technical issues, analyze feature usage, and improve our services through analytics
  • Feature Management: To enable or disable features dynamically and provide personalized experiences through feature flags
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and security violations
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to provide and improve the App. These services may collect and process your information as described below:

3.1 Infrastructure & Database

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication services. Privacy Policy

3.2 AI & Chat Services

  • xAI (Grok): AI-powered chat responses. Your messages and birth chart context are sent to xAI's API to generate personalized astrological insights. Privacy Policy

3.3 Subscription Management

  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase and subscription management. Processes your subscription status and purchase information. Privacy Policy

3.4 Authentication Services

  • Google Sign-In: Google account authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Sign in with Apple: Apple ID authentication. Privacy Policy

3.5 Notifications

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notification delivery. Privacy Policy

3.6 Error Monitoring

  • Sentry: Error monitoring and crash reporting (production only). Privacy Policy

3.7 Analytics and Feature Flags

  • Statsig: Analytics event tracking and feature flags. We use Statsig to understand how users interact with the App and to enable/disable features dynamically. Statsig collects user events, user identifiers, subscription status, and onboarding status. This data helps us improve the App experience and understand feature usage patterns. Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers listed above who perform services on our behalf
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice in the App)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly consent to share your information

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • Cloud Storage: Supabase (PostgreSQL database) and Railway (conversation history)
  • Local Device Storage: Cached conversation history and preferences stored securely on your device

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Database encryption at rest
  • JWT token-based authentication with ES256 encryption
  • Secure storage for sensitive credentials
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

Important: While we strive to protect your personal information, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after account deletion to comply with legal obligations
  • Chat History: Retained while your account is active to provide conversation continuity
  • Diagnostic Data: Error logs and crash reports retained for debugging and service improvement
  • Subscription Data: Retained for legal and accounting purposes as required by law

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

You can access your personal information through the App's settings. To request a copy of your data in a portable format, contact us at mark@mediakey.io.

7.2 Correction

You can update your profile information, birth chart data, and preferences directly in the App.

7.3 Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data through the App's settings. This action is irreversible and will permanently delete your:

  • Account information
  • Birth chart data
  • Chat history
  • Preferences and settings

Note: Some information may be retained in backups for a limited period and as required by law.

7.4 Notifications

You can manage push notification preferences in your device settings or through the App's settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 17, please contact us immediately at mark@mediakey.io, and we will delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, and shared
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information, so opt-out is not applicable
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at mark@mediakey.io.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at mark@mediakey.io. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Sending you an email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the App after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
