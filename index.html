About Luna

Luna is a mobile application that brings astrology into the modern age. Using advanced artificial intelligence, Luna provides personalized astrological insights based on your unique birth chart.

Features

  • AI-Powered Chat: Have natural conversations about astrology, relationships, career, and personal growth
  • Personalized Birth Charts: Get detailed natal chart calculations based on your birth date, time, and location
  • Multiple Profiles: Create birth charts for yourself and others to explore compatibility and relationships
  • Daily Insights: Receive personalized astrological guidance tailored to your chart
  • Secure & Private: Your personal information and conversations are protected with enterprise-grade security
