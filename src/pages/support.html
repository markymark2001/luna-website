Get Help with Taia

We're here to help you get the most out of your Taia experience. Whether you have questions about features, subscriptions, or need technical assistance, we're ready to assist.

Contact Support

For support inquiries, please reach out to us via email:

Email: mark@mediakey.io

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days.

What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you more quickly, please include the following information in your support request:

  • Device Information: Your device model and operating system version (e.g., iPhone 14, iOS 17.2)
  • App Version: Your Taia app version (found in Settings)
  • Issue Description: A clear description of the problem or question
  • Steps to Reproduce: If reporting a bug, the steps that led to the issue
  • Screenshots: If applicable, screenshots can help us understand the issue better
