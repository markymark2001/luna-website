Who we are

A small team building a calmer way to ask what matters.

We are building Taia for people who want insight without noise. Astrology can be a useful language for timing, relationships, change, and self-understanding, but only when it points people back to their own agency.

Our work combines thoughtful product design, conversational guidance, and astrological context so reflection feels simple enough to return to every week.
